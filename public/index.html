About Our Project

Nowadays thefts are increasing and the only solution to that is a secure home system. Our model only allows authorised users to enter their homes after scanning their fingerprint. Also everyone has faced this problem one time in their lives or other! Forgetting to switch off the home appliances! Now leave that to a simple app or just your google assistant and they will do the needful of switching off your appliance whenever you want them too! How cool is that!
